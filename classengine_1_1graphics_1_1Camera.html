engine::graphics::Camera class
#include <include/engine/graphics/camera.h>

Simple 2D Camera that manages the Orthographic projection.

Contents

  • Reference
    • Constructors, destructors, conversion operators
    • Public functions

It manages three matrices internally:

  1. Projection Matrix: Maps world coordinates (e.g., 0 to 1920) to GPU space (-1 to 1). This defines your "Resolution" or "Aspect Ratio".
  2. View Matrix: Represents the Camera's position and rotation in the world. Moving the camera Right (+X) actually moves the View Matrix Left (-X).
  3. ViewProjection Matrix: The combination (Proj * View). This is what we send to the Shader to transform every vertex in one go.
